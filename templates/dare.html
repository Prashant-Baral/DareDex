Community Guidelines
What Makes a Great Dare
  • Creative and original ideas
  • Safe and harmless activities
  • Inclusive for all students
  • Clear and detailed instructions
  • Appropriate language and content
  • Respect for others' boundaries
What's Not Allowed
  • Dangerous or harmful activities
  • Illegal activities
  • Bullying or harassment
  • Inappropriate or offensive content
  • Sharing personal information
  • Discrimination of any kind
Review Process: All dares are reviewed by our moderation team before being published to ensure they meet our community standards.

Tips for Creating Great Dares

Make your dares memorable and fun for everyone!

Be Inclusive

Create dares that everyone can participate in, regardless of their background or comfort level.

Safety First

Always prioritize safety and well-being. Avoid dares that could cause harm or distress.

Be Creative

Think outside the box! The most memorable dares are often the most creative ones.
