Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. DareDex collects minimal personal data required for account creation and participation in challenges.

  • Data collected: Name, email, college name.
  • We never sell or share your personal data with third parties.
  • Data is stored securely and access is limited to authorized staff only.
You can request data deletion or updates anytime by contacting privacy@daredex.com.
Terms of Service

By using DareDex, you agree to the following terms:

  • Users must be 16+ years old or have parental consent.
  • Dares must not violate any local, state, or international laws.
  • Users are responsible for their actions and interactions on the platform.
  • We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts for policy violations.

Use DareDex responsibly and with respect for the community.

Cookie Policy

We use cookies to improve your experience on DareDex:

  • Session cookies to keep you logged in securely.
  • Analytics cookies to understand how you interact with the platform.
  • We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking personal information.
You can clear cookies from your browser anytime or disable them via settings.
Community Guidelines
What Makes a Great Dare
  • Creative and original ideas
  • Safe and harmless activities
  • Inclusive for all students
  • Clear and detailed instructions
  • Appropriate language and content
  • Respect for others' boundaries
What's Not Allowed
  • Dangerous or harmful activities
  • Illegal activities
  • Bullying or harassment
  • Inappropriate or offensive content
  • Sharing personal information
  • Discrimination of any kind
Review Process: All dares are reviewed by our moderation team before being published to ensure they meet our community standards.
